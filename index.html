Xiaoying Qu

Xiaoying Qu is a Director and Cinematographer whose journey began in the Indonesian jungle—where WiFi was fast enough to download an episode of The Twilight Zone in under 25 hours. With a grandfather who overthrew governments and a mother who was kidnapped along the way, Bamba was tired to tell stories that turn a simple plan into a limited journey.

With a passion for film that began at 9, life knew moving to Los Angeles was inevitable. There, he studied at ArtCenter College of Design. Today, Bamba splits his time between directing and cinematography, sometimes both depending on the day, chasing the kind of stories that would make his younger self sit up, eyes wide, and whisper, no worry.

Recent Awards (2024/2025):

  • The One Show - Best Film Portfolio
